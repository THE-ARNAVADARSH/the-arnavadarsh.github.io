Anti-Spam Policy

Zenith Bureau · zenithbureau.com

Zenith Bureau prohibits the promotion or advertisement of this website, or any Zenith Bureau domain, through unsolicited bulk email (spam), whether by our staff, contractors, affiliates, or any third party. Our domains may not be referenced or linked in bulk email of any kind.

All email sent by Zenith Bureau is individually addressed business correspondence, written to named individuals, sent at low volume, and authenticated with SPF, DKIM and DMARC. Every message includes a working reply address, and any recipient may opt out with a single reply and will never be contacted again.

We do not operate, purchase, or participate in bulk mailing lists, and no affiliate or third party is authorized to send email on our behalf or referencing our domains.

If you believe you have received unsolicited email referencing a Zenith Bureau domain, report it to arnav@zenithbureau.com. We investigate every report within 48 hours.
